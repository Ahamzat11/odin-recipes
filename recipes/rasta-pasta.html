Rasta Pasta

Rasta Pasta is a colorful pasta dish made with a variety of bell peppers, creamy sauce and Caribbean seasonings, inspired by an original Italian recipe, but made with more traditional Jamaican ingredients.

Ingredients Required :

  • Olive Oil)
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers(mix colours)
  • Garlic cloves
  • Jamaican Jerk Seasoning
  • Cocnut Milk
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Penne Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Optional :Prawns
  • Chicken stock

Steps

  1. Chop up vegetables and chicken breast
  2. Cook the Penne to desired texture and carry on in the meantime
  3. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, paprika and JJS
  4. Heat the olive oil on medium heat and cook chicken for 6-7 minutes
  5. Remove the chicken and set aside
  6. Put the onions and peppers in the pan and let them cook for 5 minutes
  7. Add the garlic and 1 tablespoon Jamaican jerk seasoning. Cook for 1 minute, stirring.
  8. Add chicken stock, cocont milk and chicken and let simmer to thicken up
  9. Stir in the parmesean cheese and ensure food is cooked through before removing from heat
  10. Drain the pasta and mix with the sauce
  11. Serve and Enjoy !!!!
