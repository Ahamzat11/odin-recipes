Creamy Beef Stroganoff

A quick and easy recipe for the tasy Russian dish. Often made with mushrooms and served over rice or egg-noodles this tasty dish will make you lick the plate.

Ingredients Required :

  • Egg noodles
  • Ground beef
  • 1 can fat-free condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • Garlic powder(measure to taste)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper(measure to taste)

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Saut√© ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly; 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and boil.
  4. Cook egg noodles at a boil till tnder yet firm to bite.
  5. Drain and set aside the egg egg-noodles.
  6. Drain and discard any fat from cookked beef.
  7. Stir condensed soup and garlic powder into beef and let simmer.
  8. Remove from heat and mix all together with sour cream, salt and pepper.
  9. Serve and Enjoy !!!
