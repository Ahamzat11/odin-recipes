Creamy Beef Stroganoff

A quick and easy recipe for the tasy Russian dish. Often made with mushrooms and served over rice oe egg-noodles this tasty dish will make you lick the plate.

Ingredients Required :

  • Egg noodles
  • Ground beef
  • 1 can fat-free condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • Garlic powder(measure to taste)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper(measure to taste)
